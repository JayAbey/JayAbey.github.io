Scholarship

This scholarship recognises and supports top-achieving international students commencing an undergraduate degree programme at the University of Canterbury. The scholarship is currently awarded with values of $10,000, $15,000 or $20,000. Up to twenty-five scholarships may be awarded.

Regulations

Scholarship Regulations (PDF File)

Eligibility

You do not need to apply for this scholarship: an approved Application to Enrol will be taken as the scholarship application.

The scholarship is awarded to students beginning their first year of an undergraduate degree programme who:

a. have been awarded University Entrance, or the equivalent, through studying at secondary level in New Zealand

b. have studied at secondary level in New Zealand in the year of application

c. or, in the case of a gap year, in the year immediately prior


New Zealand secondary-level students who go on an official overseas exchange during or after secondary-level study may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

Amount
Up to $9000
